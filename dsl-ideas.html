00 · Abstract

The Quest for the Right Domain

This document explores ideas of domains and fields for a Domain Specific Language (DSL) proposed as a replacement project instead of MAIS (Maliyadeva Artificial Intelligence Society) or as a project contemporary with MAIS. The selection of a domain is a hard question — especially due to the many choices available.

We seek a domain that could benefit from a DSL to solve a real problem, one that has weak existing DSL implementation — if any — and one that is feasible to prototype.

ChatGPT · Criteria

🧭 Choosing the Right Domain

  • A clear real-world use case
  • Small enough to prototype
  • Benefits from textual expressiveness
  • Not already completely solved by mainstream tools

Final view: Choose the domain you can imagine refining for a long time without getting bored.

06 · Conclusion

From Analysis to Construction

The most suitable domains for a DSL are those that exhibit a clear mismatch between human conceptual simplicity and implementation complexity.

Across all contributions, a consistent pattern emerges: domains such as physics simulations, animation, and procedural systems share well-defined rules, structured behavior, and visual outputs. The problem is no longer the lack of viable domains, but the necessity of commitment.
