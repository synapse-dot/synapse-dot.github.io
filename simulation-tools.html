Simulation Ecosystem

Practical, educational, and perfectly aligned with our vision to simulate systems and explore digital complexity.

Highly Recommended

Blender

Overview

Very approachable with immediate visual results. Huge community and tutorials.

Rigid Body Physics Fluid Simulation Smoke/Fire Cloth Procedural Modelling Animation
Great for:
  • Intro simulation workshops
  • Physics visualization
  • Game simulation concepts
  • Scientific visualization
Huge Advantage Students get visually impressive results fast, which helps club momentum.

OpenFOAM

Overview

This is real engineering-grade CFD used in aerospace, automotive engineering, research labs, and academia.

Great for:
  • Airflow simulation & Aerodynamics
  • Heat transfer
  • Computational physics
This is mathematically heavy, Linux-heavy, and harder to learn. Recommended for advanced divisions.
Value Establishes the club as technically ambitious and professionally oriented.

MuJoCo

Overview

Huge in robotics, reinforcement learning, AI research, and biomechanics.

Great for:
  • Robotics simulation
  • Balancing systems
  • Walking robots
  • Physics engines
Massive Advantage Connects simulation, physics, and AI research seamlessly.

NetLogo

Best Starter Tool

Simulate complex systems with surprisingly little code.

Disease Spread Ecosystems Traffic Economics Crowd Behaviour
Perfect for:
  • Beginner workshops
  • Interdisciplinary simulations
Context Fits the "simulate systems" idea beautifully for any background.

SimPy

Overview

Aligned with Python-heavy direction. Accessible, scriptable, and GitHub-friendly.

Simulate:
  • Queues (Banks, Hospitals)
  • CPU scheduling
  • Manufacturing systems
Educational Value Teaches events, time evolution, and software architecture.
