👤 Profile

Who I Am

I am a systems-focused developer with a passion for computational science and educational infrastructure. My work sits at the intersection of physics, mathematics, and software engineering, where I build tools that help translate complex theories into interactive, visual experiences.

Since 2026, I have been dedicated to building the MCSMS Ecosystem, a student-led organization designed to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world implementation. As the founder, I lead the technical direction and architectural standards for all society projects.

💡 Philosophy

Technical Focus

First-Principles

I believe in building from the ground up. Whether it's a physics engine or a CI/CD pipeline, understanding the core logic is essential for scalability.

Open Ecosystems

Software is more powerful when it's collaborative. I design systems that are modular, documented, and easy for others to contribute to.

Visual Science

Computational models shouldn't be black boxes. I focus on creating intuitive visualizations that reveal the underlying behavior of systems.

🏛️ Leadership

MCSMS Org

As the founder of the Simulation & Modelling Society, I have designed a modular engineering environment that supports over 40 active members. My responsibilities include:

  • Designing standardized project templates and documentation frameworks.
  • Automating workflows using GitHub Actions and custom tooling.
  • Mentoring students in computational physics and systems design.
