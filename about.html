About the
Society

A student-run society dedicated to exploring science, engineering, and computation through hands-on simulation projects.

Origin

How We Started

The Simulation & Modelling Society was founded in 2026 at Maliyadeva College by a small group of students who wanted a space to apply computational thinking beyond the lecture hall. What started as informal study sessions around physics simulations grew into a structured society with a roadmap of collaborative projects.

Mission

What We Stand For

01

Hands-On Learning

We believe the best way to understand a system is to build one. Every project starts from first principles — no black boxes.

02

Open Collaboration

Projects are owned by the whole team. Ideas, code, and credit are shared openly regardless of experience level.

03

Rigorous Curiosity

We ask hard questions and follow them carefully — whether that leads to a working model or a better-defined problem.
