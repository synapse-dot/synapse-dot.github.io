Projects

Our society is currently building its first set of computational projects. These will be developed collaboratively by members of the club.

Current Status

We are in the early development phase. Projects listed below are the first ideas we plan to build together as a team. Join us to help design, code, and refine them.

Initial Project Ideas

Projectile Motion

Real-time simulation of trajectory with air resistance.

Space-Time Visualizer

Gravitational lensing effects and bending of space-time.

Game Physics Engine

Collision detection, velocities, and force vectors.

Epidemic Model

SIR model for disease spread simulation.

Particle System

Collision system with energy conservation principles.

Climate Model

Basic simulation of temperature and CO2 trends.

Build With Us

These projects are not fixed — they are starting points. Members of the club will help design, build, and improve them together.

If you join the club, you will directly contribute to building these simulations from scratch.
