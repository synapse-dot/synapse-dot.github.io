Initial ideas

Project Pipeline

P-01

Projectile Motion

Real-time simulation of trajectory dynamics with configurable air resistance and initial conditions.

Physics · Kinematics
P-02

Space-Time Visualizer

Visual model of gravitational lensing effects and the curvature of space-time around massive bodies.

Relativity · GR
P-03

Game Physics Engine

Collision detection system handling velocities, restitution, and force vectors for interactive environments.

Engines · Mechanics
P-04

Epidemic Model

SIR/SEIR compartmental model for simulating disease spread with adjustable transmission parameters.

Biology · Systems
P-05

Particle System

N-body collision engine with energy and momentum conservation principles across particle interactions.

N-body · Thermodynamics
P-06

Climate Model

Simplified coupled atmospheric model simulating temperature, CO₂ feedback, and long-term trends.

Climate · Earth Systems
